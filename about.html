About

The aim of this website is to provide information about sites that are in Kenya but not yet recognised to the public,through this webpage we will be able to onkeypress share information about these places,their location and what to do when you visit the site. We strive to offer professional, personal attention to all your needs to make your safari truly memorable.Upon arrival at your preferred site,you will be received by one of the tour guides from the site you are visiting to. Accommodation greatly depends on your budget. There are several types ranging from luxurious 5-star hotels and luxury tented camps to basic camping for those who want to experience the Country in its raw state. We will discuss your preference and choose the best depending your budget.
